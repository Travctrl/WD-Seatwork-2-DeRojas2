🕳️ The Spot

Master of Portals & Dimensional Chaos

Once a respected scientist, Dr. Jonathan Ohnn became The Spot after a failed experiment with interdimensional portals. His body is now covered in black spots that serve as gateways to other locations and dimensions, making him one of Spider-Man's most unpredictable foes.

Signature Abilities
  • 🌀Portal Creation
  • ⚡Teleportation
  • 🌌Dimensional Travel
  • 🫥Intangibility
  • 🧠Spatial Manipulation

🕳️ About The Spot

Driven by a desire to prove himself after his transformation, The Spot is both brilliant and unstable. His powers make him a formidable threat, but his lack of confidence and erratic behavior often lead to his downfall. Despite this, his mastery of portals has challenged even the most experienced Spider-heroes.

Fun Fact

The Spot's portals are not just black holes—they are windows to a strange, white void between dimensions, giving him nearly limitless escape routes and attack angles.
